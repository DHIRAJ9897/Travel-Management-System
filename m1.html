Adventure Sports in Manali:

Manali has a mountaineering institute that organizes basic and advance training
courses in trekking, muntaineering, skiing and other water sports. It is located three kms from Manali town.
Trout and Masheer are found in the Tirthan and Sainj rivers, angling tours are organized there.
The Himachal Pradesh Fisheries Department organises an angling tournament in April and September-October.


Trekking in Manali:

Most of the treks from Manali are one day journeys, the Lamadugh Meadows, Solang Valley and the Koshla-Tree line
are the most popular of them all. The best time to trek in Manali is between April and November.
